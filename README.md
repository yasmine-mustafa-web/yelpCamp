## yelpCamp

A website that represents camps where you can add your own experience or just share yoour eview on others campgrounds.

# Feautres:

**Home Page**:Containes a simple nav and a CTA btn that directs to the campgrounds.

**All campgrounds Page**:containes all the campgrounds and the title , description , photo and loctaion of each campground submitted by the user.

**Each campground page**:represents the map of the location , price , image(optional) and reviews sections including stars.

**New campground page**:Allow you to make a new campground (using Cloudinary for storing images) ;

**Edit campground page**:Allow you to update your inputs.

**Login & Register page**:I have used PASSPORT to provide me with the hash & salt password.
